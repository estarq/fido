What is Fido?

Fido is a website connecting shelters with people willing to adopt. Its search engine makes it easy and fast to search among tens of thousands of cats and dogs by sex, breed, or location.

Is it free?

Yes, it is thanks to our sponsors - PayPal, Airbnb, and Forbes.

How can I help?

Currently, we do not need help with the site. If you would like to help, please volunteer at your local shelter.
